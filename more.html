Service Base for Studies & Consulting (SBC)

Service Base for Studies & Consulting (SBC) is based on a strategic vision that focuses on promoting development through the implementation of studies and consulting, in which we reflect the actual market needs. We partake in every step of the way during the delivery to the relevant parties; ensuring that they serve the best interest of the Yemeni people in the most effective and efficient manner. Furthermore, SB offers alternative solutions based upon scientific studies and research to help decision-makers think and act in the right way, enabling them to carry out effective programs/ projects that have a noticeable impact meeting the needs efficiently and with less costly methods.

The purpose of SB is to exclude barriers between decision-makers and beneficiaries through giving information including higher level affirmations taken through collecting, analyzing, and then delivering high-quality field data to those interested parties.

We have a strong belief that the youth is the central subject of inclusive development. Thus, SB is targeting special programs that help youth build their capacity and upgrade their skills in order to be highly qualified aiming at assisting their communities and improving them for the labor market.

SB methodology is based on independency with no identification of any party or external factors that will change its path or the results of studies conducted, thus affecting its neutrality. SB is also applies professional ethics, upholding the highest standards of credibility and transparency in all works and communications, without any discrimination or prejudice.

SB depends on highly experienced cadre and consultants, by which it can provide high quality and less costly efficiency service using tools adapted to the Yemeni environment with are cultural friendly and is sensitive to the conservative nature of the local communities.

Service Base for Logistics & Construction is a private sector Yemeni company, was established for the purpose of providing high quality services and projects in various sectors.
